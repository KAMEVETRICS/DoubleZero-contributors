Galaxy HQ

Introduction

Galaxy Digital Inc. (NASDAQ/TSX: GLXY) is a global leader in digital assets and data center infrastructure, bridging traditional finance with the digital economy while accelerating advancements in finance and artificial intelligence (AI).

Founded in 2018 by Michael Novogratz, a former Goldman Sachs partner and Fortress Investment Group co-founder, the company provides institutional-grade solutions including trading, asset management, staking, self-custody, tokenization, advisory, and investment banking services.

It operates through three main segments: Digital Assets (trading, lending, asset management), Data Centers (AI and high-performance computing infrastructure), and Treasury & Corporate.

Headquartered in New York with offices across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia, Galaxy manages over $4.3 billion in assets under management (AUM) as of August 2025 and holds a significant Bitcoin treasury of approximately 6,894 BTC.

The firm positions itself as a diversified financial services provider in the blockchain and crypto space, with a growing emphasis on AI data centers to meet surging demand for scalable compute solutions.
